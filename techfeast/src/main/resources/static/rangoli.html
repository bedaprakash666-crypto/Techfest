Rangoli Competition

About the Event

Rangoli Competition celebrates creativity, tradition, and artistic expression. Participants will design vibrant and meaningful rangoli artworks based on the theme provided by organizers. It's not just decoration — it's visual storytelling.

Rules

  • Time Limit: 2–3 Hours
  • Participants must bring their own colors/materials.
  • Design must be created on the spot.
  • No pre-made designs allowed.

Team Specifications

  • Team of 2 Members.
  • Valid College ID Mandatory.

Important Guidelines

  • Use of harmful or permanent colors is prohibited.
  • Area must be cleaned after completion.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.
