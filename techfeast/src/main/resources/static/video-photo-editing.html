About Us

E-Flair 2k26 is the flagship Technical Fest of the Department of ECE & EEE at CUTM, Paralakhemundi. We bridge the gap between theoretical circuit design and real-world industrial application.

Our Mission

  • Learning by Doing: Hardware-first approach.
  • Fostering innovation in Green Energy & AI.
  • Creating a platform for future engineers.

Key Events

Technical Arena

  • Project Expo: Innovation showcase.
  • Hackathon: 24hr intensive coding.
  • Robo-War: High-octane mechanical battle.

Non-Technical

  • Gaming Arena: FreeFire & BGMI.
  • Treasure Hunt: Campus-wide mystery.
  • Reels Challenge: Creativity on camera.
