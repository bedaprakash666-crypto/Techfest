Hardware Redesign

About the Event

Hardware Redesign is a hands-on technical event that challenges participants to demonstrate their practical knowledge and precision in basic Electrical and Electronics experiments.

The event will be conducted across three laboratories: BEE Lab, EDS Lab, ECE Lab-3 and Electrical Machine Lab. Participants will perform fundamental experiments such as rectifier circuits, bridge rectifiers, and other basic practical setups.

Participants who complete experiments with maximum accuracy within the given time will be declared winners.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Use of unfair means or external help is strictly prohibited.
  • Each task must be completed within the specified time limit.
  • Safety protocols in the lab must be followed at all times.
  • Judges’ decision based on accuracy and speed will be final.

Categories

  • Electrical Circuits
  • Electronics Circuits
  • EEE Special: DC Motor Connections
  • EEE Special: Transformers Tests
  • ECE Special: Analogue and Digital Experiments

Participation Specs

  • Maximum 2 members per team.
  • Open to all Engineering students.
  • Participants must carry a valid College ID card.
