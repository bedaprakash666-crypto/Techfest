Treasure Hunt

About the Event

Treasure Hunt is an exciting on-campus adventure where teams solve clues and decode puzzles. This event tests intelligence, teamwork, observation skills, and speed. Only sharp minds reach the final clue.

Rules

  • No use of internet unless permitted.
  • Clues must not be damaged or removed.
  • Any unfair means leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Respect campus property and other teams.
  • Organizers' decision will be final.

Format

  • Multiple Clue Rounds
  • Puzzle-Based Challenges
  • Location-Based Tasks
  • Final Treasure Spot
  • Clues must be solved sequentially (no skipping allowed)

Team Specifications

  • Team of 3 Members
  • Valid College ID Mandatory

Judging Criteria

  • Fastest Completion Time
  • Accuracy of Clue Solving
  • Team Coordination
  • Rule Compliance
