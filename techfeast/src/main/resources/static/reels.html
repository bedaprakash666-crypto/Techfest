Reels Challenge — Digital Drive

About the Event

Reels Challenge — Digital Drive is a creative social media competition designed to showcase campus life, academic excellence, and student experiences through short-form video content. Participants must create engaging reels highlighting different aspects of Centurion University.

Reel Specifications

  • Duration: 30–60 seconds per reel
  • Format: Vertical (9:16)
  • High-resolution video (1080p recommended)
  • Must be original content

Submission Requirement

Each group must submit 5 reels based on the following themes:

  • Why Centurion University
  • Why ECE @ CUTM
  • Why EEE @ CUTM
  • Skill @ Centurion
  • Project Experience @ Centurion

All five reels are mandatory.

Team Specifications

  • Maximum 4 members per team
  • Valid college ID mandatory
