Tug of War

About the Event

Tug of War is a test of strength, coordination, and teamwork. Victory does not come from one strong individual — it comes from synchronized effort and unity. One team. One rope. One goal.

Rules

  • Teams must pull only after official signal.
  • No sitting or sudden dropping allowed.
  • Proper sports shoes required.
  • Any misconduct leads to disqualification.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Team Specifications

  • 5 Members per Team + 1 Extra Player
  • Valid College ID Mandatory
  • No substitution allowed (unless permitted by organizers)

Event Format

  • Knockout Rounds
  • Semi-Finals
  • Grand Final
  • Best of 3 pulls in each round
