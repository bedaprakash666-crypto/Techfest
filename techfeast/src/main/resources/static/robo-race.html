Robo War

About the Event

Robo War is an exciting competition where participants design and build robots capable of racing on a specially designed track. The robot that completes the track in the shortest time while overcoming obstacles wins the race.

Rules

  • Robot must operate within defined dimensions.
  • Only 1 out of 4 wheels may leave the track.
  • Unsafe mechanisms lead to disqualification.
  • Second chance may be allowed if participation is low.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Event Format

  • Practice Round
  • Qualification Round
  • Final Timed Run
  • Best timing from official runs will be considered.

Team Specifications

  • Maximum 3-4 members per team.
  • Teams must bring their own robot.
  • Manual wired or wireless control allowed.
