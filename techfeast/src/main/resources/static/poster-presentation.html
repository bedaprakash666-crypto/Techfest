Technical Poster Presentation

About the Event

Technical Poster Presentation provides a platform for students to visually present innovative ideas, concepts, and research work. Participants will explain their posters to judges and demonstrate their technical knowledge through discussion and interaction.

Rules

  • Content must be original.
  • Plagiarism leads to disqualification.
  • Posters must be prepared by participants.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Categories

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • IoT & Embedded Systems
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Robotics & Automation
  • Sustainable Technology
  • Emerging Technologies

Presentation Format

  • 5–7 minutes presentation.
  • 2–3 minutes Q&A session.
  • Participants must explain technical aspects clearly.
