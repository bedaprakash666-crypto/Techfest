Chess Championship

About the Event

Chess Championship is a battle of strategy, patience, and precision. Participants must outthink, outmaneuver, and outplay their opponents in a competitive tournament setting. Every move matters. One mistake can cost the game.

Rules

  • Standard chess rules apply.
  • Touch-move rule compulsory.
  • No electronic devices allowed.
  • Players must report before match time.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Participation

  • Individual Event
  • Only 1 Person Allowed per Entry
  • Valid College ID Mandatory

Event Format

  • Knockout / Swiss Format (as decided)
  • Rapid Format (time control announced before event)
  • Final round for top players

Judging Criteria

  • Match Wins
  • Tie-Break Points
  • Time Management
  • Final Round Performance
