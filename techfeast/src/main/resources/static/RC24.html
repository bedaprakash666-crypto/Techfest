About the Event

Step into the digital arena of RC24. This high-stakes mobile video gaming event challenges your reflexes, strategy, and technical prowess. Whether you are a casual gamer or a pro, RC24 is the place to prove your skills.

  • The use of unfair means, hacks, or third-party modifications is strictly prohibited.
