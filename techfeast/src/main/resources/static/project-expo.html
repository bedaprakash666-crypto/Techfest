Project Expo — Innovation Showcase

About the Event

Project Expo — Innovation Showcase provides a platform for students to present innovative hardware and software solutions. Participants will demonstrate working models and applications highlighting creativity, engineering skills, and real-world problem solving.

Rules & Regulations

  • Projects must be developed by the participants.
  • No copied or plagiarized models allowed.
  • Teams must demonstrate their project during evaluation.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Categories

  • Hardware Projects
  • Software Applications
  • IoT & Embedded Systems
  • AI / ML Based Solutions
  • Cybersecurity Tools
  • Automation & Smart Systems

Team Specifications

  • Individual participation allowed.
  • Carry a valid college ID card.
