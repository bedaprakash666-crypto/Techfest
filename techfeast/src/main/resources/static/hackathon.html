Hackathon — Build the Future

About the Event

Hackathon — Build the Future is an innovation-driven development challenge where participants design and build impactful technological solutions for real-world problems. Teams will prototype, implement, and present their ideas before a panel of judges during the fest.

Rules

  • Problem statements released 2 weeks before the event.
  • Teams must bring their project prototype.
  • Templates allowed; fully completed projects not allowed.
  • Solutions must show innovation, feasibility, and implementation.
  • Plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Final round will be held during the fest.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Categories

  • Open Innovation
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Web Development
  • App Development
  • IoT Solutions

Team Specifications

  • Maximum 4 members per team.
  • Cross-branch participation is allowed.
  • Participants must carry valid college ID cards.
