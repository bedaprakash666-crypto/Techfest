Ideathon — Innovate to Patent Challenge

About the Event

Great companies start with powerful ideas. Great patents start with bold thinking. Ideathon is a strategic innovation challenge where participants conceptualize breakthrough solutions to real-world problems with the potential for patent filing and commercialization. This event focuses on problem identification, originality, feasibility, and innovation depth. No coding required — thinking is your weapon.

Rules

  • Ideas must be original.
  • Previously patented concepts are not allowed.
  • Teams must submit a 2–3 page abstract before presentation.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.

Categories

  • Smart Technology & Automation
  • AI / Emerging Tech
  • Sustainability & Green Innovation
  • Healthcare & Assistive Tech
  • Cybersecurity & Digital Safety
  • Social Impact & Rural Innovation

Team Specifications

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Individual participation is allowed.
  • Participants must carry valid college identity cards.
